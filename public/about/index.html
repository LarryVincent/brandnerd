About

January 20, 2019

Brand Nerd is the blog of Larry Vincent. Larry is the Chief Brand Officer at United Talent Agency and a marketing professor at the Marshall School of Business at USC. He is the producer and co-host of The Findings Report, a podcast and blog about consumer psychology and why people buy, like and share. He is the author of two books, Legendary Brands (2002) and Brand Real (2012).

Brand Nerd is a free-form blog where Larry posts essays, findings and snippets from his work in consumer research and as a data scientist.
