The Artist and The Scientist

February 10, 2019

I’ve made a career out of categorizing and labeling things. That’s what branding is, when you get right down to it. Whether it’s a widget or an experience, your goal as a brand strategist is to help people think about a product or service in a certain categorical context, and then associate it with a distinctive name and identity. I’ve been doing this for over 25 years. That’s why I find it funny that I struggle to categorize and label myself.
